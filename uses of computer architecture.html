uses of computer architecture

Computer engineers test computer components and systems by developing and designing memory devices, processors, circuit boards and networks. They make computers more mobile or integrate computers in other areas, such as machines or building materials. They solve problems that arise in computer hardware and help in the advancement of computer technology.Computer engineers use models (working or theoretical) that are designed with computer simulation to test, construct and modify product prototypes. Develop and design support for peripherals and computer hardware, microprocessors, custom integrated circuits, or disk drives. The computer engineer monitors the operation of the operating system and makes any modifications or modifications to the operating system.They remain up-to - date on current developments in computer technology and apply any new knowledge to the job. He or she may also supervise the installation and manufacture of computer-related equipment. They have expertise in computers and electronics, technology and engineering, design, mathematics and physics. They contribute to artistic creativity and ideas that help in the development or design process. A lot of computer engineers work in research labs and this career test and build computer models.
